# AI Knowledge

AI automatically suggests Wiki page creation and updates. This is an admin-only feature.

## How It Works

Content is gathered from the following sources, and AI proposes changes to the Wiki:

- **File uploads** — Uploaded documents
- **Meeting transcriptions** — Meeting minutes
- **Periodic scans** — Scheduled information gathering

## Review Workflow

1. AI generates suggestions (new page or page update)
2. Admins review the suggestion list
3. Review each suggestion and **approve** or **reject** (with a reason)
4. Approved suggestions are automatically applied to the Wiki

## Filtering

Filter by status (pending, approved, rejected) or source type.
