# Getting Started

This application is an internal platform that supports team collaboration.

## Key Features

- **Wiki** — Team knowledge base for creating, editing, and sharing pages
- **AI Chat** — Conversational AI assistant with context-aware responses
- **Meetings** — Video conferencing with automatic transcription
- **Coworking** — Virtual office space for always-on team connection
- **AITuber** — AI character live streaming
- **Search** — Cross-feature search across Wiki and chat history
- **AI Knowledge** — AI-powered Wiki page suggestions (admin only)

## Navigation

Access each feature from the sidebar on the left. Admins will see an additional "Admin" tab.

## Settings

Click your avatar in the top-right corner to change personal settings such as language and theme.
